About Me

Thanks for stopping by my website! Here's a little bit about myself and what I've been working on.

Originally an aerospace engineering major, I switched to software engineering after I immediately fell in love with programming and knew I wanted to pursue a career in technology.

Throughout school, I had a few opportunities to intern at some awesome companies and decided to pivot my career towards the direction of program management after I discovered I loved the process of being able to plan and execute projects while working with a ton of different people and building relationships.

My current work resides at Google, where I've been able to combine my passion for program management and love for technology into an opportunity as a technical program manager on the Google Payments Privacy & Security team. I'm super excited to be bringing my innovate perspective of program management to the team and to make a lasting impact on the Google Privacy ecosystem.
